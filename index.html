Our Story

Rooted in craft, shaped by care

Piper & Riley Studios started at a kitchen table in 2019 — two sisters, one skein of wool, and a shared belief that handmade things carry something no machine can replicate.

We work slowly and intentionally, choosing natural fibres and traditional patterns that have been passed down through generations. Every order is made with the recipient in mind.
